I had a little nut tree,


Nothing would it bear


But a silver nutmeg


And a golden pear.


The king of Spain's 'daughter


Came to visit me.


And all for the sake


Of my little nut tree,


I skipped over water,


I danced over sea,


And all the birds in the air


Couldn't catch me.
